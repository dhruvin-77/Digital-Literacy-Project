## Project Overview

This repository contains my Digital Literacy Project, where I explored essential digital skills needed in today’s online world.

The project includes tasks such as creating an infographic on digital literacy, building a professional online presence, practicing coding and collaboration platforms, writing professional emails, and understanding cybercrime and online safety.

Through this work, I gained a better understanding of how to use digital tools effectively, communicate responsibly, and stay safe while using the internet.


---


## Project Structure

The repository is organized into the following folders:

- **task-1-presentation/** – Contains the digital literacy infographic and its description  
- **task-2-portfolio/** – Includes screenshots of GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle profiles  
- **task-3-platforms/** – Contains coding platform activity, Google Form screenshots, and notes  
- **task-4-email-etiquette/** – Includes professional email drafts and social media checklist  
- **task-5-cybercrime/** – Contains cybercrime case study and prevention checklist  
- **report/** – Final project report document

## Module Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
Created a one-page infographic using Canva covering key topics such as digital literacy, safe internet practices, and useful digital tools. The infographic was designed using structured sections, icons, and minimal text to ensure clarity and visual appeal. This task helped in understanding how complex information can be presented in a simple and engaging way.

---

### Task 2 – Digital Portfolio
Built and updated profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to establish a professional online presence. Each platform serves a different purpose, such as showcasing projects, networking, and practicing skills. This task helped in understanding how a well-maintained digital profile can support academic and career growth.

---

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Completed a beginner-level coding problem on HackerRank to practice problem-solving skills and basic programming logic. In addition, created a Google Form titled "Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz" to understand how collaboration tools can be used to collect and manage data. This task highlighted the importance of online platforms in learning and teamwork.

---

### Task 4 – Email Etiquette & Social Media Guidelines
Drafted two professional emails for academic and internship purposes, focusing on proper structure, tone, and clarity. Also created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts to promote responsible online behavior. This task emphasized the importance of professional communication and maintaining a positive digital presence.

---

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Developed a case study on UPI fraud explaining how such scams occur, who is targeted, and their consequences. Also created a prevention checklist with practical and actionable tips to help users stay safe online. This task helped in understanding common cyber threats and the importance of digital awareness and security.
