## Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

### Do’s
- Maintain a professional and respectful profile
- Share useful and positive content
- Use strong privacy settings to protect your data
- Verify information before sharing
- Think before posting anything online

---

### Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
- Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or arguments
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links
- Do not spread false or unverified information
