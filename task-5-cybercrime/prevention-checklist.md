## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
- Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve unexpected payment requests, especially when someone says you will receive money.
- Always verify the identity of the sender before making any online payment or clicking on links.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts for extra security.
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts and change them regularly.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions or accessing sensitive accounts.
- Download apps only from trusted and verified sources such as official app stores or well-known websites.
- Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history for any suspicious activity.
- Be cautious of messages or calls offering rewards, cashback, or urgent account issues.
- Immediately report any cyber fraud on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the helpline number- 1930.
